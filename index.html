Story

From the galli of Handiguan to the city of Dallas with a vision

Raised between late-night studio session and bedroom speakers, NAWAJ ANSARI built a sound that sits somewhere between industrial shadows and gospel glow. No label, no backing — just an independent artist turning every show into a temporary universe.

The SAMRAJYA Tour is the first full-scale run: a series shows where energy, crowd, and live vocals collide in one monochrome fever dream. Every city gets a unique intro, custom visuals, and limited City-Specific Merch.

From a small room studio to sold-out cities — the mission stays the same: keep it REAL, keep it 1 HUNDRED, and move in SILENCE.
